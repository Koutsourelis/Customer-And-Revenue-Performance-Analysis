
# 🍿 Customer & Revenue Performance Analysis  
### 📊 Business Intelligence Case Study using SQL & Excel
> 🚀 An executive-level business analytics project focused on customer behavior, revenue performance, and operational insights for a multi-store DVD rental company.

# 📌 Executive Summary

This project analyzes customer behavior, revenue distribution, and operational performance for a multi-store DVD rental business using SQL and Excel.

The objective was to provide management with actionable insights regarding:

✅ Customer Value  
✅ Revenue Diversification  
✅ Rental Demand  
✅ Store Performance  
✅ Customer Activity Trends  

The final deliverable was an executive-style dashboard designed to support data-driven business decisions in:

- customer retention
- revenue optimization
- inventory planning
- operational monitoring

---

# 🎯 Business Problem

The company lacked visibility into:

❌ Which customer segments generated the most revenue  
❌ How revenue was distributed across stores and countries  
❌ Whether customer activity and rentals were concentrated or diversified  
❌ Which operational areas could support future growth  

Management needed a centralized performance overview to support:

📈 Customer Retention Strategy  
💰 Revenue Optimization  
📦 Inventory Planning  
🏪 Store Performance Evaluation  

---

# ❓ Business Questions Answered

### 🔍 Customer & Revenue Analysis
- Which customers generate the highest revenue?
- Which countries contribute most to sales performance?
- Is revenue concentrated among a small number of customers?

### 🎬 Rental Performance
- How balanced is rental demand across films and categories?
- Which film categories perform best?

### 👥 Customer Activity
- Are inactive customers creating lost revenue opportunities?
- How consistently are stores performing?

---

# 🛠️ Analysis Approach

Using the Sakila relational database, transactional rental and payment data were analyzed through SQL queries and transformed into an executive-level Excel dashboard.

### 📌 Analysis Areas
- Revenue Trends
- Customer Contribution
- Rental Activity
- Store Performance
- Geographic Revenue Distribution
- Customer Activity Status

The dashboard was designed to simulate a real-world management reporting environment.

---

# 📊 Key KPIs

| KPI | Description |
|---|---|
| 💰 Total Revenue | Overall sales performance |
| 🎟️ Total Rentals | Number of completed rentals |
| 👤 Avg Revenue per Customer | Customer value measurement |
| ⏱️ Avg Rental Duration | Rental behavior analysis |
| 🟢 Active vs 🔴 Inactive Customers | Customer engagement tracking |
| 🌍 Revenue by Country | Geographic sales performance |
| 🏪 Store Performance | Operational comparison |

---

# 🔍 Key Findings

## 🌍 Customer Distribution

Revenue was strongly correlated with customer concentration by country, indicating that markets with larger active customer bases generated significantly higher sales.

---

## 💸 Revenue Stability

Revenue contribution was relatively diversified across customers, reducing dependency on a small group of high-value accounts.

✅ Lower customer concentration risk  
✅ More stable revenue streams  

---

## 🎬 Rental Demand

Film rentals were evenly distributed across categories and titles, suggesting balanced customer demand and lower inventory concentration risk.

📦 Strong catalog diversification  
📈 Consistent rental activity across products  

---

## 🏪 Operational Performance

Store-level performance remained relatively stable, indicating consistent operational execution across locations.

---

## 👥 Customer Activity

Inactive customers represented a potential untapped revenue opportunity for re-engagement campaigns.

⚠️ Potential churn indicators identified  
📢 Opportunity for retention-focused marketing initiatives  

---

# 💡 Business Recommendations

## 👥 Customer Retention

Implement customer reactivation campaigns targeting inactive users through:
- personalized offers
- loyalty incentives
- targeted promotions

---

## 🌍 Geographic Expansion

Prioritize marketing investments in high-performing countries where customer concentration already drives strong revenue performance.

---

## 🎬 Inventory Strategy

Maintain a diversified film catalog to:
- support broad rental demand
- reduce dependency on specific movie categories
- improve customer variety

---

## 📈 Performance Monitoring

Introduce recurring KPI monitoring for:

✅ Customer Activity  
✅ Repeat Rentals  
✅ Store Performance  
✅ Revenue Concentration  

to improve long-term operational visibility and strategic decision-making.
